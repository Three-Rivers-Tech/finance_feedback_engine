## Repair/Service Work Management

**Philosophy:** Necessary evil, not growth driver. Time-box strictly.

### Rules:
1. **10 hours/week maximum** on service work (repairs, house calls)
2. **$500 minimum** for house calls (filter out low-value)
3. **Prepayment required** (reduces no-shows, time-wasters)
4. **Automated scheduling** (online booking, no phone tag)
5. **Knowledge base first** (most issues solved via docs)

### Pricing strategy:
- **House calls:** $150 base + $100/hr (premium pricing filters clients)
- **Remote support:** $75/hr (encourage remote over on-site)
- **Repairs:** Parts + 50% markup + $100 labor minimum
- **Consulting:** $200/hr (enterprise clients only)

### Automation:
- Online booking system (Calendly or custom)
- Diagnostic flowcharts (customer self-diagnoses)
- Parts ordering automation (customer orders, we configure)
- Follow-up automation (post-service surveys, upsells)
